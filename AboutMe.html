TOP 45 Film-making Talent

TOP 45 may not sound particularly fancy, but it marked my first challenge upon entering high school. This contest, organized by RMIT and discovered through Facebook, prompted me to create a group with individuals who shared my passion for participating. Successfully advancing through the introduction round and the subsequent round, we found ourselves on the brink of entering the final stage. Unfortunately, we fell short.
