My Hometown

Phu Yen Province located in the central coastal region of Vietnam and it features stunning coastal landscapes, including pristine beaches like Bai Xep and Bai Mon. Its capital, Tuy Hoa, offers a vibrant cultural scene and delicious cuisine. With historical sites and scenic attractions, Phu Yen is a must-visit destination for travelers seeking both relaxation and adventure.

WHAT CAN WE EAT HERE?

Due to its location in the central coastal region, the cuisine of Phu Yen is a fusion of coastal and traditional dishes. It boasts diversity, with seafood taking center stage, featuring fresh catches from the nearby ocean incorporated into many recipes. Here are some must-try foods when visiting Phu Yen.

Bánh Xèo is a beloved dish by its crispy rice flour pancake filled with savory ingredients. It is a popular choice among locals and visitors alike for its delightful combination of textures and flavors.

Bánh Bèo is a delightful Vietnamese dish consisting of small, round steamed rice cakes topped with savory ingredients such as shrimp, pork skin. It is a popular choice among locals and visitors for its delicious taste and simplicity.

Cơm Gà or chicken with rice, is a local specialty for its delious flavours and comforting simplicity. This dish features tender chicken cooked with rice and other vegetable.

TOURIST ATTRACTION

  • Gành Đèn

    This scenic wonder, located near Gành Đá Đĩa, is known as Gành Đèn, though it may not enjoy the same level of fame. The main reason for this is the challenging accessibility, making it less frequented by visitors. However, for those adventurous enough to venture there, Gành Đèn offers a captivating coastal experience, featuring rugged cliffs, panoramic sea views, and a historic lighthouse.

  • Tháp Nhạn

    This building locates in Phú Yên Province, which is a historic tower that stands as a symbol of the region's rich cultural heritage. Dating back centuries, this ancient structure showcases intricate architectural details and serves as a testament to the area's historical significance. Tháp Nhạn attracts visitors with its captivating design and the stories it holds within its walls.

  • Gành Đá Đĩa

    Gành Đá Đĩa is a nature wonder because it is known for its unique geological formations. It was formed by many hexagonal basalt columns, this stunnong landscape is a sight to behold. This is a mus-visit destination for natur lovers.

  • Mũi Điện

    This scenic coastal area is a bit further from the capital, yet it boasts beautiful natural surroundings. When visiting here, you can explore its pristine beaches, observe local fishing activities, and immerse yourself in the tranquility of this coastal paradise.
